# Measurement Modes and Timing  
测量方式与测量时机

This document explains the two orthogonal dimensions of measurement
in the Compassion as Measurement framework:

- how a measurement intervenes in the possibility space
- when a measurement should intervene

本文件用于说明「慈悲的测量」框架中两条独立但相互配合的维度：
测量方式（如何介入）与测量时机（何时介入）。

---

## Overview

In this framework, a measurement is not defined by the outcome it produces,
but by how and when it intervenes in the possibility space.

在本框架中，测量不是由结果定义，
而是由其介入可能性空间的方式与时机所定义。

Two orthogonal axes are used:

- Measurement mode (how to intervene)
- Measurement timing (when to intervene)

---

## 1. Measurement Modes  
测量方式（How to measure）

Measurement modes describe how a judgment intervenes in the structure
of the possibility space.

测量方式描述判断如何介入并改变可能性空间的结构。

Three fundamental modes are defined.

---

### 1.1 Exclusion measurement  
排除式测量

Exclusion measurement removes certain paths from the possibility space.

It answers the structural question:

Which options should no longer be considered selectable?

排除式测量通过判断，将某些路径从可能性空间中移除。

其核心不是做出选择，而是决定：
哪些路径不再构成选择。

Structural effect:

- the boundary of the possibility space is reduced
- uncertainty is intentionally compressed

结构效果：

- 缩小可能性空间边界
- 以确定性换取聚焦

---

### 1.2 Opening measurement  
打开式测量

Opening measurement reintroduces or reveals paths that were previously
invisible, ignored, or prematurely excluded.

打开式测量将原本不可见、不可想象，或被过早排除的路径，
重新纳入可能性空间。

Structural effect:

- the possibility space is expanded
- false binary structures are broken

结构效果：

- 扩展可能性空间
- 打破“非此即彼”的结构幻觉

---

### 1.3 Restructuring measurement  
重构式测量

Restructuring measurement does not add or remove options.
Instead, it changes the evaluative structure by which options are judged.

重构式测量并不直接增减路径，
而是改变判断轴、优先结构与不可交换的判断原则。

Structural effect:

- the relative meaning and priority of paths are changed
- the decision landscape is reshaped

结构效果：

- 重排判断优先级
- 改变可能性空间的“地形坡度”

---

## 2. Measurement Timing  
测量时机（When to measure）

Measurement timing determines whether an intervention should occur now
or should be postponed.

测量时机用于判断：
是否应当在当前情境下对可能性空间进行介入。

Two timing modes are defined.

---

### 2.1 Immediate measurement  
即时式测量

Immediate measurement intervenes in the possibility space at the current moment.

即时式测量指在当下立即进行结构性判断介入。

It is appropriate when:

- key paths have already emerged
- noise does not dominate judgment

适用于：

- 关键路径已充分展开
- 噪音未主导判断

---

### 2.2 Deferred measurement  
延迟式测量

Deferred measurement intentionally postpones structural intervention.

延迟式测量指有意识地推迟判断介入，
以避免在高噪音或路径尚未展开的情境下，
使可能性空间过早坍缩。

Its purpose is not to delay decisions,
but to protect the possibility space
until meaningful paths become visible.

其目的不是拖延决策，
而是保护尚未展开的可能性空间。

---

## 3. Relationship between modes and timing  
方式与时机的关系

Measurement modes and measurement timing are independent dimensions.

测量方式与测量时机是两条相互独立的轴。

- modes describe how the structure is modified
- timing determines when modification is allowed

测量方式回答：
如何改变地形。

测量时机回答：
是否应当现在改变地形。

Deferred measurement does not replace exclusion, opening, or restructuring.
It only controls when these modes should be applied.

延迟式测量并不取代排除式、打开式或重构式测量，
而仅决定这些方式是否应当在当前时刻被使用。

---

## 4. Core principle  
核心原则

Timing precedes mode.

时机先于方式。

A decision should first determine whether intervention is appropriate,
before determining how intervention should occur.

任何判断都应先决定是否介入，
再决定如何介入。

---

## One-line summary

Measurement modes describe how the possibility space is changed.  
Measurement timing determines when such change should occur.

测量方式决定如何改变可能性空间，  
测量时机决定何时改变可能性空间。
