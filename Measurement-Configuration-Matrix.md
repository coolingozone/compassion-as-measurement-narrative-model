# Measurement Configuration Matrix  
测量配置判定矩阵

This document defines how to determine
when and how a measurement should intervene
in the possibility space.

本文件用于判定：
在特定情境中，应当何时、以及以何种方式介入可能性空间。

---

## Purpose｜目的

This matrix does not decide which outcome should be chosen.

It determines:
- whether an intervention should take place now, and
- how the intervention should reshape the possibility space.

本矩阵不用于决定应选哪一个结果，
而用于决定：

- 是否应当进行判断介入
- 以及应如何改变可被选择的结构。

---

## Two Axes｜两条判定轴

The matrix is formed by two independent dimensions.

本矩阵由两条相互独立的维度构成。

---

### 1. Timing axis（测量时机）

- Immediate measurement（即时式测量）
- Deferred measurement（延迟式测量）

This axis determines whether structural intervention
should occur at the current moment.

该轴用于判断：
是否应当在当前时刻介入可能性空间。

---

### 2. Mode axis（测量方式）

- Exclusion measurement（排除式测量）
- Opening measurement（打开式测量）
- Restructuring measurement（重构式测量）

This axis determines how the possibility space
is structurally modified.

该轴用于判断：
应当以何种结构方式介入可能性空间。

---

## Step 1 – Decide the timing  
第一步：判定测量时机

Answer the following two questions.

请先回答以下两个问题：

### Q1. Have the key possible paths sufficiently unfolded?

关键路径是否已经充分展开？

If no → Deferred measurement  
若否 → 延迟式测量

---

### Q2. Does current noise dominate judgment?

当前噪音是否可能主导判断？

If yes → Deferred measurement  
若是 → 延迟式测量

---

Only if both answers are no, choose:

只有当以上两题皆为否时，才选择：

→ Immediate measurement（即时式测量）

---

## Step 2 – Decide the mode  
第二步：判定测量方式

After timing is determined, examine the structural nature of the problem.

在确定时机后，再判断问题的结构性质。

---

### Case A — Should certain paths be removed?

是否存在必须被明确移除的路径？

Typical signs:

- clearly unacceptable
- clearly irreversible
- violates non-negotiable constraints

→ Exclusion measurement（排除式测量）

---

### Case B — Is the possibility space overly compressed?

是否被困在过度收缩的选择空间中？

Typical signs:

- false “either–or” structure
- relevant actors or consequences are systematically ignored

→ Opening measurement（打开式测量）

---

### Case C — Has the evaluation structure itself failed?

判断结构本身是否失效？

Typical signs:

- options remain similar
- but existing judgment criteria no longer produce meaningful distinctions

→ Restructuring measurement（重构式测量）

---

## Measurement Configuration Matrix  
测量配置矩阵

| Timing \ Mode | Exclusion | Opening | Restructuring |
|--------------|-----------|---------|----------------|
| Immediate    | Immediate Exclusion | Immediate Opening | Immediate Restructuring |
| Deferred     | Deferred Exclusion  | Deferred Opening  | Deferred Restructuring  |

---

## Interpretation of the six configurations  
六种配置说明

### Immediate Exclusion
Immediately removes unacceptable paths to rapidly narrow the space.

### Immediate Opening
Immediately reintroduces overlooked paths to break structural narrowing.

### Immediate Restructuring
Immediately changes the evaluation structure and priority axes.

---

### Deferred Exclusion
Postpones removal of paths until the space has sufficiently unfolded.

### Deferred Opening
Allows possible paths to emerge before deliberately expanding the space.

### Deferred Restructuring
Observes how the existing structure fails under pressure before redesigning it.

---

## Core principle｜核心原则

Timing precedes mode.

时机先于方式。

A judgment should first determine whether intervention is appropriate,
before determining how intervention should be performed.

任何判断，应先决定是否介入，
再决定如何介入。

---

## One-line summary

The matrix determines when and how to intervene in the possibility space,
not which outcome should be selected.

本矩阵用于判定：
何时、以及以何种方式改变可被选择的地形，
而非决定最终答案。
