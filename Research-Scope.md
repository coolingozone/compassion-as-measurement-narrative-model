# Research Scope  
# 研究范围

## Overview  
## 概述

This research develops a narrative decision model to formalise how structurally consistent decisions can emerge within a defined narrative possibility space.

The model provides a framework for describing how characters navigate uncertainty, constraints, and external forces while maintaining internal coherence across long-form narrative structures.

This research is conducted as part of the development of the novel *The Awakeners* (《觉醒者》), and focuses on establishing the structural foundation that governs narrative logic and character transformation.

本研究旨在建立一种叙事决策模型，用于形式化描述在一个既定的叙事可能性空间中，结构一致的决策如何产生。

该模型提供一个框架，用于描述角色如何在不确定性、约束条件和外力作用下作出选择，同时在长篇叙事中保持内在结构一致性。

本研究作为小说《觉醒者》的结构基础开发的一部分，重点在于确立支撑叙事逻辑与角色转变的结构性原则。

---

## Research Questions  
## 研究问题

This research investigates the following questions:

- How can a narrative possibility space be defined and described in structural terms?  
- How does measurement timing affect the collapse of narrative possibilities into specific outcomes?  
- How can structurally consistent decision pathways be maintained across long-form narrative development?  
- How can external forces, constraints, and uncertainty be formally incorporated into narrative decision structures?

本研究探讨以下问题：

- 如何以结构性方式定义和描述叙事的可能性空间？  
- 测量时机如何影响叙事可能性的坍缩与具体结果的形成？  
- 如何在长篇叙事发展过程中维持结构一致的决策路径？  
- 如何将外力、约束与不确定性形式化地纳入叙事决策结构中？

---

## Methodology  
## 研究方法

The research adopts a structural and operational approach, including:

- Formal definition of core elements such as possibility space, measurement, external forces, and noise  
- Development of operational measurement configurations and decision pathways  
- Documentation of structural logic and decision models in a formalised framework  
- Experimental application of the model in narrative scenarios and controlled decision environments

研究采用结构性与操作性方法，包括：

- 对可能性空间、测量、外力与噪音等核心元素进行形式化定义  
- 建立可操作的测量配置与决策路径模型  
- 将叙事结构逻辑记录为可复用的形式化框架  
- 在叙事场景与受控决策环境中进行实验性应用与验证

## Research Nature  
## 研究性质说明

This research focuses on developing a structural narrative model as a creative methodology. It does not seek to predict or control narrative outcomes, but to formalise the structural conditions under which coherent narrative decisions emerge.

本研究旨在发展一种结构性叙事模型，作为创作方法论的一部分。本研究并不试图预测或控制叙事结果，而是形式化描述结构一致的叙事决策如何产生的条件。
